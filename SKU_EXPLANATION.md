# üìù What is SKU? - Complete Explanation

## SKU Definition

**SKU** stands for **Stock Keeping Unit**. It's a unique identifier code assigned to each product in your inventory.

---

## üéØ Purpose of SKU

### 1. **Unique Product Identification**
- Each product gets a unique SKU code
- Helps distinguish between similar products
- Example: "IPH15PRO-128GB-BLK" vs "IPH15PRO-256GB-BLK"

### 2. **Inventory Tracking**
- Quick lookup of products
- Easier stock management
- Faster product searches

### 3. **Business Operations**
- Helps with ordering and reordering
- Simplifies sales and reporting
- Useful for barcode scanning (if implemented)

---

## üìã SKU Examples

### Electronics
- `IPH15PRO-128GB-BLK` - iPhone 15 Pro, 128GB, Black
- `SAMSUNG-S23-256GB-BLU` - Samsung S23, 256GB, Blue
- `LAPTOP-DELL-XPS15-512GB` - Dell XPS 15, 512GB

### Clothing
- `TSHIRT-M-L-BLK` - T-Shirt, Medium, Large, Black
- `JEANS-LEVI-32-34-BLU` - Levi's Jeans, Waist 32, Length 34, Blue

### Food Items
- `COKE-330ML-CAN` - Coca Cola, 330ml Can
- `BREAD-WHITE-500G` - White Bread, 500g

---

## ‚úÖ Best Practices for SKU

### 1. **Keep it Short but Descriptive**
- ‚úÖ Good: `IPH15-128-BLK`
- ‚ùå Bad: `iPhone15ProMax128GBBlackColor`

### 2. **Use Consistent Format**
- Use dashes or underscores to separate parts
- Example: `BRAND-MODEL-SIZE-COLOR`

### 3. **Make it Meaningful**
- Include key product attributes
- Avoid random numbers like `PROD-12345`

### 4. **Avoid Special Characters**
- Use only letters, numbers, dashes, and underscores
- ‚úÖ Good: `PROD-ABC-123`
- ‚ùå Bad: `PROD@ABC#123`

### SKU Field Details
- **Field Name:** SKU (Stock Keeping Unit)
- **Required:** No (Optional)
- **Location:** Product form, below Product Name
- **Placeholder:** "e.g., IPH15PRO-128GB-BLK"

### When to Use SKU
- ‚úÖ When you have many similar products
- ‚úÖ When you need quick product lookup
- ‚úÖ When you want better inventory organization
- ‚úÖ When planning to use barcode scanning

### When SKU is Not Needed
- ‚ùå Small inventory (few products)
- ‚ùå Simple product names are enough
- ‚ùå Products are very different from each other

4. **Keep Records:** Document your SKU format for reference
